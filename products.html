Products and Services

Boresha Loan

This is a loan facility evaluate both formal groups and individual members who run income generating.BORESHA means to improve or make it better.

Boresha Plus Loan

Boresha Plus is a loan facility available to both groups and individual members to address short financial needs.This loan is processed within one hour after application. .

Dharura Loan

This is an individual self secured loan facility which is available to all members to address short term emergency business challenges.This loan is processed within twelve hours after successful application.

Taji Loan

This educational loan(school fees loan)facility which is available for secondary schools,colleges and universities fees.The facility is available for the whole term e.g secondary school years,college and university until cover expires.

Thamani Loan

This is an asset finance loan that gives access to business and individual to access assets such as equipment,machinery,vehicles etc.

Usoni Loan

This is a loan facility for a formal group specifically for investment projects and income generating activity to help members earn additional income.

Mkataba Loan

This is a loan facility available to persons who have a contract or tender to supply and do not have enough finance to support the tender or contract.This loan depends on the duration of the contract to tender and the amount required.

kilimo biashara Loan

This is an agribusiness loan facility available to large-scale and small scale agribusiness persons specifically for farming or agricultural based activities.

Bima Loan

This is an insurance premium loan facility available for all comprehensive insurance only.This loan is payable within one agricultural based activities.

Wekeza Loan

This is an opportunity available to all members only for capital placement in order to gain profitable returns as interest ,income or appreciation in value.
