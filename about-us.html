Who We Are?

Total Finance Empowerment Programme is a non deposit taking Micro finance.Registered under Companies Act(CAP 486).We provide Credit facilities through groups,individuals and unbanked client in Kenya.Our current Geographical focus is Rural and Sub-Urban in Kenya.

Our Mission?

To improve the living standards of our clients through provision of affordable,flexible,competitive and effectual credit concepts.

Our Vision

To be a modern,vibrant financial solutions provider that transforms the economical activities of poor people in Kenya.
